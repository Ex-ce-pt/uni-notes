
[+](Thermodynamics#Work) Constant temperature, reversible expansion of an ideal gas:
$w = -nRT \cdot ln(\frac{V_2}{V_1})$

[+](Entropy#Entropy%20and%20volume) Constant temperature, expansion of an ideal gas:
$\Delta S = nR \cdot ln(\frac{V_2}{V_1})$
$\Delta S = nR \cdot ln(\frac{p_1}{p_2})$

[+](Thermodynamics#Heat) Constant volume, no non-expansion work:
$\Delta U = q$

[+](Enthalpy) Constant pressure, no non-expansion work:
$\Delta H = q$

[+](Thermodynamics#Heat%20capacity) Ideal gas:
$C_{p} = C_{V} + nR \Leftrightarrow C_{p, \space m} = C_{V, \space m} + R$

[+](Entropy#Global%20changes%20in%20entropy) Surroundings (any conditions):
$\Delta S_{\text{surr}} = \frac{q_{\text{surr}}}{T}$

[+](Entropy#Global%20changes%20in%20entropy) Constant temperature & pressure:
$\Delta S_{\text{surr}} = -\frac{\Delta H}{T}, \space T = \text{const}, \space p = \text{const}$
