
**Electrophile (Lewis acid)** - substance that accepts an $e^-$-pair from another substance' seeks a negative center.
**Nucleophile (Lewis base)** - substance that donates an $e^-$-pair to another substance; seeks a positive center.
**Base (Bronsted)** - abstracts a photon (donates $e^-$-pair to a proton specifically).
**Leaving group** - group that leaves the substrate and takes an $e^-$-pair with it.
**Substrate** - the molecule the substitution is operating on; the main reactant.
