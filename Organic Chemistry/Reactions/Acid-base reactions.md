
## Reaction types

**Substitution:**
$\ce{Y + R-X -> Y-R + X}$

**Addition:**
$\ce{R=R' + X-Y -> X-R-R'-Y}$

**Elimination:**
$\ce{X-R-R'-Y -> R=R' + X-Y}$

**Rearrangement:**
$\ce{X-R-R' -> R-R'-X}$

**Redox** - oxidation numbers change.

## Acid-base reactions

**Bronsted acid** - proton donor.
**Bronsted base** - proton acceptor.

**Lewis acid** - $e^-$-pair acceptor.
**Lewis base** - $e^-$-pair donor.
