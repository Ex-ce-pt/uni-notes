# Inorganic Chemistry

## Cations

> (*element*) (*oxidation number*) ion

$\ce{Fe2+}$ - iron(II) ion
$\ce{Fe3+}$ - iron(III) ion
$\ce{Na+}$ - sodium ion

## Anions

Monoatomic anions:
> (element)-ide ion

$\ce{Cl-}$ - chloride ion

Polyatomic anions:
> \[n-hydrogen] \[hypo-/per-] (element) (-ate/-ite) ion

$\ce{H2PO4-}$ - dihydrogen phosphate ion

- per-X-ate
- X-ate
- X-ite
- hypo-X-ite

## Ionic compounds

> (cation) (anion)

$\ce{KCl}$ - potassium chloride
$\ce{CoCl}$ - cobalt(III) chloride

Hydrates:
> (ionic compound) (n-hydrate)

$\ce{CuSO4*5H2O}$ - copper(II) sulfate pentahydrate

## Acids

> \[per-/hypo-] (element) (-ic/-ous) acid

Anion endings change to acid endings:
- -ate → -ic ($\ce{H2SO4}$ - sulfuric acid)
- -ite → -ous ($\ce{H2SO3}$ - sulfurous acid)

## Alkanes

By the # of $\ce{C}$ atoms:

> (# of $\ce{C}$)-ane

| Alkane   | Group (-yl) | # of $\ce{C}$ atoms |
| -------- | ----------- | ------------------- |
| methane  | methyl      | 1                   |
| ethane   | ethyl       | 2                   |
| propane  | propyl      | 3                   |
| butane   | butyl       | 4                   |
| pentane  | pentyl      | 5                   |
| hexane   | hexyl       | 6                   |
| heptane  | heptyl      | 7                   |
| octane   | octyl       | 8                   |
| nonane   | nonyl       | 9                   |

| undecane | undecyl     | 11                  |

## Alkenes

> \[$x_1,x_2...$-n]-(# of $\ce{C}$)-ene **OR**
> (# of $\ce{C}$)-\[$x_1,x_2...$-n]-ene

$\ce{C2H4}$ - ethene

## Alkynes

> \[$x_1,x_2...$-n]-(# of $\ce{C}$)-yne **OR**
> (# of $\ce{C}$)-\[$x_1,x_2...$-n]-yne

$\ce{C2H2}$ - ethyne

## Alcohols

> \[$x_1,x_2...$-n]-(hydrocarbon)-ol **OR**
> (# of $\ce{C}$)-\[$x_1,x_2...$-n]-ol

$\ce{CH3CH2OH}$ - ethanol
$\ce{CH3CH(OH)CH3}$ - 2-propanol/propane-2-ol

## Haloalkanes

> \[$x_1,x_2...$-n]-(halogen)-(hydrocarbon)

$\ce{CH3CHBrCH3}$ - 2-bromo-propane

## Alcoholates

> (cation) (hydrocarbon)-oxide

$\ce{CH3CH2ONa}$ - sodium ethoxide

## Aldehydes

> \[$x_1,x_2...$-n]-(hydrocarbon)-al **OR**
> (hydrocarbon)-\[$x_1,x_2...$-n]-al

$\ce{CH3CH2CHO}$ - ethanal

## Ketones

> \[$x_1,x_2...$-n]-(hydrocarbon)-one **OR**
> (hydrocarbon)-\[$x_1,x_2...$-n]-one

$\ce{CH3CH2CH2CH(OH)CH3}$ - 2-pentanone/pentane-2-one

## Carboxylic acids

> (hydrocarbon)-oic acid **OR**
> (acid name)-oic acid

$\ce{HCOOH}$ - methanoic acid
$\ce{CH3COOH}$ - ethanoic acid/acetic acid

## Salts

> (cation) (acid name)-ate

$\ce{CH3COONa}$ - sodium ethanoate/sodium acetate

## Ethers

> (smaller hydrocarbon)-oxy-(larger hydrocarbon)

$\ce{CH3OCH2CH3}$ - methoxyethane

## Esters

> Just like [salts](#Salts)

$\ce{CH3COOCH2CH3}$ - ethyl ethanoate/ethyl acetate

## Amine

> \[$x_1,x_2...$-n]-amino-(hydrocarbon) **OR**
> (hydrocarbon)-amine

$\ce{CH3NH2}$ - aminomethane/methylamine
## Amide

> (hydrocarbon)-amide

$\ce{CH3C(O)NH2}$ - ethanamide
