#Theme1 
Key points:
- Organic chemistry.
- Bacteria & antibiotics.
- Drug development.

What we will be doing:
Investigation of novel pharmaceuticals.

Risk assessments need to be done in advance before every lab.

Activities:
1. **[[Extraction]]** - separate a mixture of compounds.
2. **Bioassay** - test the antibacterial effect of compounds.
3. **Identification** - using spectroscopic techniques to find the structure of the molecules.
4. **Synthesis** - creation of the desired chemicals from source components.
5. **Report** - presentation of the process and the results to an audience.


Theoretical:
$$
\begin{gather}
\text{Compound \#1:} \\
M = 253.28 \space g \cdot mol^{-1} \\
m_{min} = 2 \space mg,\space m_{max} = 4 \space 4 \space mg \\
c = 20 \space mM = 2 \cdot 10^{-2} \space M \\

c = \frac{n}{V} \Rightarrow V = \frac{n}{c}=\frac{m}{c \cdot M} \\
V_{min} = \frac{2 \cdot 10^{-3} \space g}{2 \cdot 10^{-2} \space M \cdot 253.28 \space g \cdot mol^{-1}} = 0.3948 \space ml \\
V_{max} = \frac{4 \cdot 10^{-3} \space g}{2 \cdot 10^{-2} \space M \cdot 253.28 \space g \cdot mol^{-1}} = 0.7896 \space ml \\

$$
