#Science 
Importance - communication of the research results to the public orally

Core idea:
Background → Question → Methods → Results → Conclusion

First impressions matters: dress appropriately, body language etc.

Tips:
- Consider the background of the audience
- One slide = one idea
- Highlight key points
- Don't read
- Speak clearly, vary voice tone
- Make eye contact
- Make pauses
- Stick to allocated time
