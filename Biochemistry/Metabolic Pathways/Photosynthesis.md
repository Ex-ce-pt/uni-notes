## Light absorption

**Ground state** - stable state of energy.
**Excited state** - a molecule's state of energy following the absorption of a photon.
Fluorescence - light emission by returning to the ground state.

For e^- to transition to a higher energy state, it needs to absorb a photon of specific energy.

Exciton and exciton transfer - transfer of energy by absorption and reemission of a photon from one molecule to another.
Chlorophyll absorbs light and then reemits it.
that's how energy is transferred to the reaction center

Chlorophyll (green) have Mg2+ ions to absorb light
(can use Co, Fe as well for reddish color)

carotenoids - yellow, red, purple molecules absorbing photons
see in the autumn

> e^- are not absorbed! It's the photons that get absorbed!

**Action spectrum** - graph of rate of photosynthesis against the wavelength of light.

Light harvesting complex (LHC) - chlorophylls + proteins that hold them.
